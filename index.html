About

LemanTh is an annual event in Lausanne bringing together researchers in machine learning theory, high-dimensional statistics, statistical physics, and theoretical computer science. The 2026 edition continues the tradition of focused discussions on expressivity, optimization, generalization, and phase transitions in modern ML systems.
